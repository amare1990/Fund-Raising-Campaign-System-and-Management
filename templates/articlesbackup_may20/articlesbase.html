COVID Specific News About Ethiopia

#COVID19Ethiopia latest: Fourteen (14) people have tested positive for #COVID19 out of 3271 laboratory tests conducted in the last 24 hrs, bringing the total confirmed number in Ethiopia to 365.

Four (4) people have also recovered, bringing the total recovered number to 120. Of the 14 who tested positive today six (6) have travel history, seven (7) have contact traces with previous case & one (1) has neither of both. Among these nine (9) are from #AddisAbeba (Seven of these are due to contact with previous case, one is a cross country truck driver, and the other one is the one who has neither travel nor contact). One is from #Tigray, #Mekelle quarantine center, and one is from #Oromia. The patients in both cases are cross country truck drivers. The last three are from #Jigjiga quarantine center in #Somali region.

#Correction from the Ministry of Health: One case which was announced yesterday from #Ataye quarantine center in #Amhara region, is an old case which was already counted. Therefore yesterday's tally is 34, instead of 35.
